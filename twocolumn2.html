Obi-Wan Kenobi, Mace Windu and the Force Awakens

Two very skilled Jedi and a new era

Obi-Wan Kenobi is the teacher of Anakin Skywalker. Obi-Wan was born on the planet Stewjon. When he was only six months old, he was taken to the Jedi Temple on Corusant. There he was trained.

After his master Qui-Gon Jinn died, Obi-Wan became a Jedi Knight, then finally a Jedi Master. There, he got a Padawan when he was a Jedi Knight. His Padawan (Anakin Skywalker), became a Jedi as well until Anakin fell to the Dark Side. After nearly killing Skywalker on Mustufar, Obi-Wan witnessed the birth of Luke and Leia Skywalker and took Luke to Tatooine to his uncle and aunt. He was slain on the first Death Star by Darth Vader.

Mace Windu

Mace Windu was a Human Male Jedi born on Haruun Kal in 50 BBY. He was a very daring Jedi as he preffered to use the lightsaber style Vapaad, which he created. Vapaad used a combination of Light and Dark Side techniques. Very few Jedi have been able to master Vapaad because the Dark Side calls to you when you use it AND it is very hard to resist. Unlike most Jedi, Mace wields an amethyst-bladed lightsaber. In lightsaber combat, he is only bested by Yoda.

Rey

Rey is a young girl who was left on the desert planet Jakku. There, she was forced to be a scavenger and trade ship parts for food. After a few misadventures, she met Han Solo and Chewbacca and when she was captured by Kylo Ren, she was brought to Starkiller Base. There, she discovered she was Force Sensitive by using a Jedi mind trick on a stormtrooper to escape from her prison cell. Later, she witnessed the death of Han Solo who was killed by Kylo Ren, his own son. Eventually, she avenged Han by slashing Kylo Ren's face and possibly scarring his face forever.

Finn

FN-2187(Finn), was a First Order Stormtrooper. He betrayed the First Order by freeing Resistance pilot Poe Dameron and helping him escape. On the way to Jakku, the TIE Fighter that they had stolen was shot down. Mysteriously, Poe dissapeared, leaving Finn alone on Jakku. Eventually, he meets Rey and escapes Jakku with her. On Takodana, he was given Anakin Skywalker's lightsaber to give to Rey, who had ran into a forest. He witnessed Kylo Ren carry an unconsious Rey into his personal starfighter. Later, he rescues Rey from Starkiller Base and sees Kylo Ren mortally wound Han Solo and knock him into a abyss. He engages Kylo Ren in battle in the snowy forests of Starkiller Base. Kylo slashes Finn's back and lethally wounds him. Fortunately, he survives.
